Key Achievements

RFC
Approved Sep 2025

ECR Pull-through Cache — Hybrid Image Distribution Strategy

CI Platform · Zendesk

$163K+ Annual Cost Savings
2-3x Faster Image Pulls
8 AWS Regions
90%+ Storage Cost Reduction

Problem

Cross-region ECR replication caused slow image pulls (up to ~7m49s in APAC regions), high data transfer costs ($68K+/month), and storage waste from replicating images to regions where they were never used.

Solution

Architected a hybrid approach combining cross-region replication for disaster recovery (primary → DR region) with pull-through cache for all other regions. Images are cached on-demand only where used, eliminating storage waste and cross-region transfer costs.

Impact

Reduced image pull latency from 7-8 minutes to ~2-3 minutes in distant regions. Achieved over 90% storage and data transfer cost reduction per region. Designed multi-phase rollout across 7 regions with mutating webhook for zero-touch developer adoption.
